UX/UI Design: Crowdfund dApp Security Suite
I designed the core authentication and recovery flows for Crowdfund dApp, focusing on building institutional trust through a high-fidelity "Security-First" aesthetic. The project balances deep obsidian tones with a vibrant "Action Green" to guide investor interactions.

Key Contributions
Adaptive Login Experience: Developed a sleek login interface featuring Quick Access via FaceID, designed to reduce friction for returning investors while maintaining a secure posture.

Multi-Step Verification: Architected a clear, 3-step password recovery protocol that utilizes end-to-end encryption for all secure links, providing users with a sense of progress and safety.
